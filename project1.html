Seattle Pike Place Market

Pike Place Market is a public market in Seattle, Washington,
United States. It opened on August 17, 1907,
and is one of the oldest continuously operated public farmers' markets in the United States.
Overlooking the Elliott Bay waterfront on Puget Sound, it serves as a place of business for many small farmers, craftspeople and merchants.
